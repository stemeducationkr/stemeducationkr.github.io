Civic Action Project

Bridging the Regional & Experiential STEM Education Gap

The Problem

Current South Korean educational structures rely heavily on high-pressure, exam-centric paradigms. This creates severe information and accessibility bottlenecks for peripheral or underserved student populations, locking them out of vital hands-on career discovery before making irreversible academic choices.

Our Policy Solution

We advocate for decentralized, out-of-school experiential STEM and career exploration frameworks. By leveraging structural partnerships with corporate stakeholders (such as Samsung and SK Hynix) and prestigious institutions (such as POSTECH), we can systematically deliver project-based immersion to students who lack access to premium private academies.
